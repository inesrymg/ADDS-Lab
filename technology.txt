Computers process information using binary code of ones and zeros.
They store data in memory and execute instructions very quickly.
Modern computers are used in science, medicine, and communication.

The internet connects millions of computers around the world.
It allows people to share information and communicate instantly.
Data travels through cables and wireless signals across the globe.

Artificial intelligence allows machines to learn from data.
It is used in image recognition, language processing, and more.
Many scientists believe AI will transform the world in the future.
